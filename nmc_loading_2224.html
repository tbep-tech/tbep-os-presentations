RA FRAMEWORK

  • Each year, assess attainment of water quality thresholds
  • Loadings only evaluated if water quality thresholds not attained in two consecutive years
  • Annual reporting to FDEP, fourth year of 2022 – 2026 RA period

CONCLUSIONS

  • Chlorophyll-a thresholds attained for first three years of 2022 – 2026 RA period
  • No need to evaluate loadings – but, absolute load exceeded for OTB and RALTB in 2022, all segments except LTB in 2024
  • High loadings in 2024 from tropical storm activity, NPS contributions
  • Nitrogen delivery ratio not exceeded in any segment for 2022 – 2024
